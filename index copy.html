The Entrepreneurial Poem

Creative and driven Bound to succeed Seeing opportunity Where others concede

Seeking out problems To see in a new light Creating solutions Well into the night.

Failures seen as lessons Getting closer to goals Reaching new heights With them at the controls.

Knowing their limits Yet exceeding them at times With each new venture Their status now climbs.

Giving back as they go Regardless of ego The capitalist spirit In the form of a pro.

When one idea is finished Another is started Taking so much risk Is not for the faint hearted.
